3 Pro Tips to Generate Stunning AI Art

Tip #1: Be Specific with Your Prompt

The more clearly you describe your idea, the better the results. Mention mood, color palettes, style (cyberpunk, watercolor, 3D render), subject details, and even the time of day or emotional tone.

Tip #2: Guide the Composition

Don’t leave the layout to chance. Whether you want a centered portrait, a wide-angle scene, or minimalistic negative space, tell the AI what to focus on and how to arrange it.

Tip #3: Tweak. Re-try. Evolve.

Great art rarely happens on the first try—neither does AI art. Adjust your prompt, change keywords, or add more intent. Each variation reveals new possibilities, and often, the best results come from subtle refinements.
